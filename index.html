Privacy Policy

Livin Log

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Livin Log is a personal tracking app designed to help users log movies, TV shows, important dates, and personal lists. Your data belongs to you.

Data Collection

Livin Log does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. All information entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device and, if enabled, within the user’s private iCloud account.

Third-Party Services

Livin Log does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or external tracking SDKs.

Cloud Storage

If iCloud is enabled on your device, your data may be synced using Apple’s CloudKit services. Livin Log does not have access to your Apple ID or iCloud account.

Children’s Privacy

Livin Log does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
